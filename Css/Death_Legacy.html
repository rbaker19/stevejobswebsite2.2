End of Time (for Steve) 1975-2011

Steve Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011 from pancreatic cancer at 56 years old. He battled it for almost a decade. He passed away in Palo Alto, California. Apple did not stop just because he passed away.

After his death Apple released many Iphones, Ipods, Macs, Apple TV and the Apple Watch. Apple will continue to release new and innovative products, even though the company's co-founder is no longer with us.
